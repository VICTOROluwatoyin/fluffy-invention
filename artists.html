Meet Our Talented Artists

Discover the incredible talents behind the Official AfroVybz movement

Mark6ix Okpani

Mark6ix Okpani is the leading artist of Official AfroVybz, celebrated for his impressive discography, which includes standout projects like "247 Mark of Greatness" and "Move Like Mark." Collaborating with notable figures such as Jujifina and prominent South African producers Telemetro and Dekalemo, as well as Nigeria's Mr. Kleff, Mark6ix has established himself as a prolific hit-making machine.

His infectious tracks, including "Afro Vybz or Nothing," "Metete," and "Solo," showcase his unique sound and creativity in the Afrobeat genre. Mark6ix continues to push boundaries and create music that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Notable Tracks:
  • "Afro Vybz or Nothing"
  • "Metete"
  • "Solo"
  • "247 Mark of Greatness"
  • "Move Like Mark"

Dilo Swags

Dilo Swags is another remarkable artist contributing to the AfroVybz movement, serving as the lead singer for the brand's official anthem. Known for his close collaboration with Jujifina and LQDENT, Dilo Swags has made a significant impact with tracks like "Ajungele to the World," "Thunder," and the theme song "AfroVybz."

His diverse talents promise exciting future projects, including an upcoming EP that is highly anticipated by fans of the genre. Dilo Swags brings a unique energy and vocal prowess that perfectly complements the AfroVybz sound.

Notable Tracks:
  • "AfroVybz" (Official Anthem)
  • "Ajungele to the World"
  • "Thunder"
Upcoming:

New EP - Highly anticipated by fans

Telemetro

Telemetro, hailing from South Africa, stands out as one of the most formidable producers in the AfroVybz movement, recognized for his artistic prowess and innovative sound. His partnership with vocal producer Jujifina has proven fruitful, as they work together to craft captivating songs that often feature Jujifina's original melodies.

Telemetro's ability to marry strong beats with compelling arrangements has solidified his reputation as a leading figure in the Afrobeat production scene. His South African roots bring a unique flavor to the AfroVybz sound, creating a perfect blend of African musical traditions.

Specialties:
  • Afrobeat Production
  • Beat Making
  • Song Arrangement
  • Collaboration with Jujifina
Origin:

South Africa - Bringing authentic African sound

Rocksteady Raheem

Rocksteady Raheem is a prominent Nigerian artist celebrated for his innovative blend of Afrobeat and reggae. Evolving from Nigeria's vibrant musical landscape, he has established himself as a leading figure in the industry, known for his engaging performances and socially aware lyrics.

Raheem's career received a significant boost when he was signed under the legendary Afrobeat pioneer 2Baba (formerly 2Face Idibia) and his company, 2Tech. This affiliation provided Raheem with invaluable exposure and mentorship, helping him to hone his craft and reach a wider audience.

In a notable collaboration, Raheem teamed up with the talented artist Jujifina on her movement called "Official AfroVybz," also known simply as AfroVybz. Together, they produced the highly anticipated track "Day and Night," also referred to as "Painkiller," which showcases their combined artistry and rhythmic synergy. The song has garnered significant attention, resonating with fans and further solidifying Raheem's position in the Afrobeat genre.

Rocksteady Raheem's music reflects his commitment to addressing personal and social themes, drawing inspiration from his experiences and the realities of everyday life in Nigeria. With his distinctive voice and lyrical talent, he continues to engage audiences both locally and internationally, paving the way for a new generation of artists while promoting the Official AfroVybz movement.

Notable Achievements:
  • Signed under 2Baba/2Tech
  • Collaboration with Jujifina
  • Blend of Afrobeat and Reggae
  • Socially conscious lyrics
Notable Tracks:
  • "Day and Night" (Painkiller)
  • Various socially aware tracks
Origin:

Nigeria - From the vibrant musical landscape
