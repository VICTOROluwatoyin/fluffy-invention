About Us

Jujifina is a dynamic vocal producer, recording artist, and the executive behind LQD ENT. She is also the innovative founder of Official Afro Vybz (OAV), a cultural movement that transcends traditional entertainment by fostering inclusion, education, and love through music and dance. As a polyglot and culture enthusiast, Jujifina connects with diverse audiences around the globe. Her collaborations with artists such as Flama de la Section, Eddy Ranks, Temptation (Tentacion), Mark6ix, and Rocksteady reflect her versatility and commitment to artistic excellence.

  • Executive of LQD ENT
  • Collaborations with top international artists
  • Manager of rising stars like Dilo Swagz, Mark6ix Okpani, Cigity, Winna Boy, and Rocksteady

Mission and Vision: Jujifina's mission is to bridge cultural gaps and bring people together through the universal language of music. Her vision is to create a global community where music and culture inspire and unite people from all walks of life.

01 Who is Jujifina?

Jujifina is a vocal producer, recording artist, executive of LQD ENT, and the founder of Official Afro Vybz (OAV). She is known for her diverse skill set, including music production, artist management, and brand development.

02 2. What is Official Afro Vybz (OAV) ?

Official Afro Vybz (OAV) is a cultural movement founded by Jujifina. It aims to showcase music, culture, and dance while promoting inclusion, education, and love through the diaspora connection.

03 What makes Jujifina’s approach unique?

Jujifina’s approach is unique due to her multilingual abilities, cultural enthusiasm, and extensive experience in the music industry. Her passion for connecting with diverse audiences and her commitment to artistic excellence set her apart.
